“I want you to create an image with a top-down view of a lake for my video game.”

“I want you to create an image with a top-down view of the inside of an abandoned tower for my video game.”

Tools/Models used: ChatGPT (GPT 5.2), Gemini 3 (Nano Banana & Nano Banana Pro), Recraft (V2 & V3), Flux AI (Flux Schenell & Flux 2 Pro), Stable Diffusion XL Base 1.0



=============================================TEXT GENERATION=============================================

“Create a short dialogue between the king Arithmos of Numeria and the hero (e.g. Alexander) that finally returned the magic scepter to him. I want the hero reassuring the king that the scepter is safe and the king to thank him for completing his challenging mission.”

“The hero enters the castle. Create a short description of it, max 50 words.”

“The hero interacts with a locked door that he needs to unlock with a weapon, in order to continue his journey. Create a short narration for this interaction. Max 50 words.”

“Create a short narration about how the hero feels before he has to fight the evil magician Enigmor. Make sure that he sounds anxious and scared, but determined at the same time.”

“Can you create a short description about each one of these rooms inside the castle?” + image as input


Tools/Models used: ChatGPT (GPT 5.2), Gemini 3 Nano Banana Pro, DeepAI (DeepSeek V3.2 & Llama 4 Scout), Toolbaz (ToolBaz-v4.5-Fast & Claude-Sonnet-4 by Anthropic), Voila (OpenAI GPT-4), Picsart AI Writer
